THE STORY BEHIND US....

Siruthuli has been a people's movement since its inception in 2003 with the focus to Clean Kovai and Green Kovai and has worked relentlessly to restore water bodies, increase the green cover in the region that we have lost due to urbanization, create awareness among school children and citizens and manage both solid and liquid waste.
